Brand Story
The pennies are disappearing.
One by one, the world's pennies are going out of circulation.

But deep in a hidden workshop, five friends run the last PennyMint — the only place still making pennies.

Each penny they press holds a lesson. Each lesson sparks an adventure. And every kid who joins becomes a PennyMinter — a keeper of the coins the world forgot.

The pennies aren't just money. They're stories waiting to be told.
